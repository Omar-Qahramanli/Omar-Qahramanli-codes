Currency control in practice

How to get money for services from foreign clients

I am a programmer, I develop websites. In November 2018, I was approached by a German startup. We discussed the terms and agreed that they would transfer the payment in dollars to me on check.

What is currency control?

In Russia, all foreign exchange transactions are subject to regulation. When a payment comes from abroad, the Government and the Central Bank must be sure that the money is received legally.

>

To accept payments in foreign currency, you need to open a foreign currency account.
